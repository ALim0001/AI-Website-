Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with End Plastics?

We have various volunteer opportunities available, from participating in beach cleanups to helping with educational programs. To get started, fill out our volunteer application form on our website or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@endplastics.org.

How can I donate to support your initiatives?

You can make a donation through our website by clicking on the "GIVE" button in the navigation menu. We accept various payment methods including credit cards and PayPal. For larger donations or corporate partnerships, please contact our development team at donations@endplastics.org.

Do you offer educational resources for schools?

Yes, we provide curriculum materials, classroom activities, and virtual presentations for schools at all levels. Our education team works with teachers to develop age-appropriate materials about plastic pollution and conservation. Contact education@endplastics.org for more information.

How can my company partner with End Plastics?

We offer various partnership opportunities including corporate sponsorships, cause marketing campaigns, employee engagement programs, and consulting services for sustainability initiatives. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@endplastics.org to discuss collaboration opportunities.
